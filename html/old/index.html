How to contribute

Using MyEtherWallet

1.Click on Send Ether & Tokens

2.Access your wallet and log in

3.In the "To Address" field, fill in the ICO contract address: 0x----------

4.We strongly advice to use at least 300,000 as gas limit to make sure your transaction will be processed by the contract. Unused gas will be returned to you.

5.You can usually lower gas price to 7-8 Gwei and still get your transaction processed in a short time.

6.Press Generate Transaction followed with Send Transaction

7.Once the transaction is mined you will automatically receive your VGC tokens

8.Press "Add Custom Token" under "Token Balances" in the right column

9.In the "Address" field, fill in the token contract address: 0x----------

10 In the "Token Symbol" field, fill in VGC

11In the "Decimals" field, fill in 3

12.Press Save. You should now be able to see your token balance

Using MetaMask

1.Make sure you've imported your account

2.Press Send

3.In the "Recipient Address" field, fill in the ICO contract address: 0x

4.In the "Amount" field, fill in the amount you wish to send. Minimum accepted is 0.01 VibGyor Coin

5.Press Next

6.We strongly advice to use at least 300,000 as gas limit to make sure your transaction will be processed by the contract. Unused gas will be returned to you.

7.You can usually lower gas price to 7-8 Gwei and still get your transaction processed in a short time.

8.Press Submit

9.Once the transaction is mined you will automatically receive your VGC tokens

10 Head to the "Tokens" tab and press the "+" at the bottom of the window

11In the "Token Address" field, fill in the token contract address: 0x----------

12.In the "Token Symbol" field, fill in VGC

13.In the "Decimals of Precision" field, fill in 3

14.Press Add. You should now be able to see your token balance
